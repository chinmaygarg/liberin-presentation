## Company Overview

**Liberin Technologies** is building India's enterprise AI stack — a vertically integrated platform where **Septa** provides intelligence, **Boliye** enables AI interactions, and **PiiVacy** ensures governance and compliance.

- **Stage:** Bootstrapped, 18+ months R&D

## Products

### Septa — Enterprise GenAI Analytics / Decision Intelligence

Enterprise-grade generative AI platform for analytics and decision intelligence. Enables natural language queries over enterprise data, transforming how organizations extract insights and make decisions.

### Boliye — Conversational AI Voice Agents / Contact Center Automation

Multilingual conversational AI platform powering voice agents for enterprise contact centers. Built for India's vernacular-first market with support for regional languages.

### PiiVacy — Privacy Governance & DPDP Compliance Platform

Full-stack privacy governance platform purpose-built for India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023. Automates consent management, data mapping, DPIAs, and compliance workflows.
